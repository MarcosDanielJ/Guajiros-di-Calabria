About us:

The Guajiros di Calabria Team is made up of students from the University of Calabria who attend the courses of Electronic Engineering and Mechanical Engineering, united by a passion for aerospace engineering. We are dedicated to the development of innovative projects, research and collaboration in the technological field, with the aim of creating a dynamic environment for growth and learning. In addition to developing technical skills, we look to the future with ambition, focusing on entrepreneurial initiatives, innovation and engineering applications that can have a concrete impact in the aerospace and technological sector.
